Exploring Climate Change

The computer model below simulates the effect of cloud cover and carbon dioxide (CO2) on the temperature of earth. To start the simulation, click on the Start/Stop button. The CO2 is shown as green and white molecules in the atmosphere. To run the simulation again, press the Reset button. A graph above the simulation shows the global temperature over the time.

Heat Transfer in Metal Experiment

A materials scientist wants to understand how heat transfers through a metal bar. She attached four temperature sensors evenly along the length of the bar as shown in the picture. She then applied a heat source to one end of the bar.
She wrote this computer program to record temperature readings from the sensors: 
repeat forever:
   value1 = readSensor(1)
   value2 = readSensor(2)
   value3 = readSensor(3)
   value4 = readSensor(4)
   print 1, value1, time
   print 2, value2, time
   print 3, value3, time
   print 4, value4, time

   pause 20 seconds

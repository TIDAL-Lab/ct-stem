Temperature in Bacteria Growth Experiment

A biologist wants to understand the growth of bacteria in four different temperature conditions. She uses four different sensors to measure a chemical toxin produced by the bacteria cultures.
She wrote this computer program to record temperature readings from the sensors: 
repeat forever:
   value1 = readSensor(1)
   value2 = readSensor(2)
   value3 = readSensor(3)
   value4 = readSensor(4)
   print 1, value1, time
   print 2, value2, time
   print 3, value3, time
   print 4, value4, time

   pause 20 seconds
